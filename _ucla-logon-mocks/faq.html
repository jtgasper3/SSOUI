Help

Having trouble signing-in?

To sign-in to this application, you need to have a UCLA Logon ID.

  • I don't have a UCLA Logon ID. I need one.
  • I can't remember my password.

I have a UCLA Logon ID/password but I can't sign-in.

If you've had trouble signing in with your UCLA Logon ID, try reopening your browser and signing in again.

If you continue to experience problems with your UCLA Logon ID account, or have questions about your UCLA Logon ID account, please contact the Bruin Online Help Desk at (310) 267-HELP (4357).
